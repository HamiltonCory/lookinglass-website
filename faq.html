Get answers to the most common questions about Lookinglass, the first pay-as-you-go AI client that puts you in complete control.

Why pay-as-you-go instead of subscriptions?

We believe in subscription fatigue relief. Instead of paying $20-200/month whether you use AI or not, you only pay for the messages you actually send. Top up your wallet with amounts from $1-500, use what you need, keep the rest. Many users spend less than $5/month this way.

How much does it typically cost to use?

Costs vary by model and message length, but here are some examples:

  • Simple questions (GPT-4o-mini): $0.001-0.01 per message
  • Complex reasoning (o3): $0.01-0.10 per message
  • Premium reasoning (o3-pro): $0.05-0.50 for complex tasks

The app shows you the exact cost before sending each message, so there are never surprises.

What happens to unused wallet funds?

Your wallet funds never expire and are non-refundable. You can use them whenever you want, even months later. There are no monthly fees or account maintenance charges.

Can I get a refund on wallet funds?

Wallet funds are non-refundable once added, as stated in our terms. However, they never expire, so you can use them whenever you're ready.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

Key differences:

  • Model Choice: You pick the exact model; ChatGPT routes automatically
  • Transparency: See costs upfront; ChatGPT pricing is hidden
  • Control: Adjust memory, context windows; ChatGPT is fixed
  • Access: Get o3-pro without $200/month Pro subscription

What's special about o3-pro access?

o3-pro is OpenAI's most advanced reasoning model, normally exclusive to ChatGPT Pro subscribers ($200/month). Lookinglass gives you access on a pay-per-message basis, so you can use it for important tasks without the monthly commitment.

What does "Flex pricing" mean?

Flex pricing saves you 50% on message costs if you're willing to wait a bit longer for responses. It's perfect for non-urgent tasks where you want to optimize for cost over speed.

ðŸ“± App Features

What is "permanent memory" and how does it work?

Permanent memory lets you tell the AI key information about yourself once, and it remembers across all conversations. For example: "I'm a Python developer working on iOS apps." This context gets included automatically, so you don't need to re-explain your background each time.

Can I control how much the AI remembers?

Absolutely! You control the context window:

  • Short memory: Remember just the last few messages (cheaper, faster)
  • Long memory: Remember the entire conversation history
  • Custom: Set exactly how many messages to remember

More context costs more but provides better continuity.

What is the cost guard feature?

Cost guard alerts you before sending messages that exceed your chosen threshold. Set it to $0.01, $0.10, or any amount, and you'll get a warning before accidentally sending an expensive message.

Can I use voice input?

Yes! Lookinglass includes built-in voice-to-text, so you can speak your questions naturally instead of typing them.

Which devices are supported?

Lookinglass works on iPhone and iPad running iOS 15 or later. It's designed exclusively for Apple devices.

Does it work offline?

No, Lookinglass requires an internet connection to communicate with AI models. However, your conversation history and settings are stored locally when possible.

Can I export my conversation history?

You can export your transaction history and usage data as CSV files. Individual conversations aren't stored on our servers for privacy, but you can copy/save them locally within the app.
