Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

At Lookinglass, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app that provides a GUI for the OpenAI API.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple ID, we receive limited information from Apple, which may include:

  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (if you choose to share it)
  • A unique identifier from Apple

Usage Metadata

We collect minimal metadata necessary for app functionality and billing:

  • Which AI model you selected for each request
  • Timestamps of when requests were made
  • Cost estimates and actual costs for each request
  • Whether you used Standard or Flex pricing
  • Effort level settings for O-models (Small/Medium/Large)
  • Token usage statistics

Financial Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through in-app purchases. We do not store your payment methods or financial information. We only track:

  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Purchase amounts and timestamps
  • Usage charges against your wallet balance

What We Don't Collect

We do not store, log, or have access to:

  • Your conversations or chat content
  • Your prompts or messages to AI models
  • AI model responses or outputs
  • Your permanent memory settings content
  • Any personal data within your conversations

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Service Operation: Facilitating API requests to OpenAI
  • Billing: Calculating costs and managing your wallet balance
  • Support: Providing customer assistance when requested
  • App Improvement: Understanding usage patterns to improve the app (in aggregate only)

Data Sharing

OpenAI

Your prompts and conversations are sent directly to OpenAI's API. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

Apple

Payment processing and account authentication are handled by Apple. Your financial transactions are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

No Other Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties for marketing or other purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Minimal data retention practices
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls limiting who can view account information

Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Account Information: Until you delete your account
  • Transaction History: 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Usage Metadata: 2 years for service improvement and support
  • Conversations: We don't store these at all

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your transaction history as CSV
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@lookinglass.ai.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you a notification through the app (if significant changes)

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@lookinglass.ai

Lookinglass is committed to protecting your privacy while providing you with powerful AI tools.
