How Our Pricing Works

Our pricing is based on the actual computational cost of your requests:

Model Used

Premium models like o3-pro cost more than basic models like GPT-5-nano

GPT-5-nano (Flex): $0.06 input / $0.50 output per 1M tokens
GPT-4o: $6.25 input / $25 output per 1M tokens
o3-pro: $50 input / $200 output per 1M tokens

Service Tier

Standard for immediate responses, Flex for exactly 50% savings when you can wait

Flex available: GPT-5, GPT-5-mini, GPT-5-nano, o3, o4-mini
Standard only: GPT-4o, o1, o3-pro, and other models

Context Window

Control how much conversation history the AI remembers. Disable for unrelated questions like "How big was the Titanic?" Keep enabled to remind the AI of recent conversation - disabling it gives the AI amnesia where every message feels like your first interaction.

Message Length

Longer messages and responses use more computational resources. A simple "What's 2+2?" costs much less than "Write a detailed business plan for a coffee shop."
